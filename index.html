ABOUT

CORD Comms was born in December 2022 and within less than a year secured  40+ features in publications and segment-specific media, hosted 2 UGC-boasting influencer events, and crafted 12+ media strategies.
We are passionate about leveraging new tech for a better world and dedicated to supporting femtech projects across the globe.
Our team's extensive expertise enables seamless navigation between startup dynamics and the luxury sector, fostering cross-pollination of best practices and innovative ideas.

Maria MacDonald
London, UK

Maria is the founder and director of CORD Comms agency where she takes the lead in nurturing partnerships, developing brand assets and managing marketing projects for our lifestyle and tech clients. Drawing on her extensive experience in the beauty and luxury FMCG sectors, Maria stays ahead of trends by leveraging insightful expertise and creative campaigns. She's worked with an impressive roster of renowned brands such as La Mer, Jo Malone London, McQueens Flowers and Aveda. Maria also co-owns the premium salon CULt. Hairdressing based in the heart of Spitalfields, London where she, naturally, takes the helm of the marketing activity from social media curation to press relations.

Fun fact - Maria is also a Wing Chun martial artist.

Darya Bihun
Valencia, Spain

Darya manages the PR team here at CORD Comms and is responsible for the strategic development of clients: their positioning in the media, activities and ideas for publications and events. With a degree in journalism and having worked for many years as a journalist and news presenter on television, Darya seamlessly combines her work with writing articles and interviews for digital media. This rich journalistic experience has been imperative to Darya building authentic and successful relationships with press, understanding editorial policies and being unafraid to be creative and come up with unconventional ideas.
